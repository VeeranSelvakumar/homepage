Privacy Policy (English)

Veeran Tech Point is a private online digital service centre. We help customers apply for government certificates and services from their homes without visiting government offices.

Information We May Collect

  • Name and contact details
  • Required document details (with customer consent)

Purpose of Data Usage

  • Preparing and submitting government applications
  • Verification and correction of application details
  • Providing customer support and updates
We do not misuse or sell customer data. Sensitive information is handled securely and used only for service purposes.
