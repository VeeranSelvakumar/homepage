SSB Recruitment 2026 – 827 Constable Posts

Organization: Sashastra Seema Bal (SSB)

About Recruitment

SSB (Sashastra Seema Bal), one of India’s border security forces, has announced recruitment for Constable posts. A total of 827 vacancies are available under this recruitment.

Post: Constable

Total Vacancy: 827

📅 Important Dates

  • Application Start: 21 March 2026
  • Last Date: 20 May 2026

🎓 Eligibility

  • 10th Pass (Minimum)
  • Technical posts require ITI
  • Indian Citizen

🎂 Age Limit

  • 18 – 23 / 25 Years
  • SC/ST/OBC Relaxation Available

💰 Salary

  • ₹21,700 – ₹69,100 (Level 3)
  • Allowances included

🧪 Selection Process

  • Physical Test (PET/PST)
  • Written Exam
  • Trade Test
  • Medical Test
  • Document Verification

🏃 Physical Test

  • Running Test
  • Height & Chest Measurement
  • Long Jump / High Jump

✍️ Signature Rules

  • Use clear signature

  • No overwriting
