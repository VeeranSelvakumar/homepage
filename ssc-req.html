SSC Phase XIV 2026 Notification – Full Details

This page provides complete information about SSC Phase 14 2026 Selection Posts Recruitment. Candidates are advised to read all details carefully before applying.

📅 Important Dates

  • Application Start: 13 April 2026
  • Last Date: 04 May 2026 (11 PM)
  • Fee Payment Last Date: 05 May 2026
  • Correction Window: 11–13 May 2026
  • Exam Date: June 2026 (Tentative)

📢 Vacancy Details

Various Selection Posts are available across Ministries and Departments of Government of India. Vacancies are tentative and may change at any time.

🎓 Eligibility

  • Must be Indian Citizen
  • Qualification depends on post:
    • Matriculation (10th)
    • Higher Secondary (12th)
    • Graduation & above

🎂 Age Limit

Age varies depending on post category (generally 18 to 30 years).

  • SC/ST: 5 years relaxation
  • OBC: 3 years relaxation
  • PwBD: 10 years relaxation

💰 Application Fee

  • General: ₹100
  • Women / SC / ST / PwBD: No Fee

🖥 Exam Pattern

  • Total Questions: 100
  • Total Marks: 200
  • Duration: 60 Minutes
  • Negative Mark: 0.5

📚 Syllabus

  • General Intelligence (Reasoning)
  • General Awareness
  • Quantitative Aptitude
  • English Language

🧾 Selection Process

  • Computer Based Examination (CBE)
  • Document Verification

📄 Required Documents

  • 10th Certificate
  • Educational Certificates
  • ID Proof (Aadhaar / Voter ID / Driving License)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • PwBD Certificate (if applicable)
  • Photograph

⚠️ Important Instructions

  • Application will be accepted provisionally
  • Wrong information leads to rejection
  • Upload documents after exam
  • Only one application per post allowed

🚫 Rejection Reasons

  • Incorrect details
  • Blurred photo/signature
  • Multiple applications
  • Fake documents
